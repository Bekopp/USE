Structural

USE provides quality structural engineering services to both private and public sector clientele throughout southern Ontario. We deliver value-added engineering solutions together with exceptional service while upholding our values of reliability, professionalism, and client satisfaction.

Building Structural Engineering

USE specializes in the structural design of Mid-rise residential buildings ranging from one to six levels including Modular and Integrated Building System.

USE has developed a proprietary design program that takes into account the following parameters in its design:

  • rigid and flexible diaphragms
  • wind and earthquake loads
  • consideration for non-combustible stair exits
  • calculates inter-storey drift and overall building drift
  • considers cumulative effect of hold-down slippage
  • database of 35 different hold down anchors and 250 shear wall combinations.

Restoration Structural Engineering

USE has successfully completed a large number of structural investigation and restoration projects for owners, municipalities, condo corps, and contractors across Ontario.

Our technical experience as structural engineers allows us to understand complex building systems, assess their performance, and identify signs of distress. Whatever the issue, USE will work with the stakeholders to evaluate the condition of the existing structure, or specific building element, and determine how best to remediate any damage that is discovered.
